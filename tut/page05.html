05: Dinnertime

Ada rejoins the conversation, a little impatiently. "Are we about ready to eat? What are we having?" it asks.

Brick replies, "I got no idea, I just want some Food. Any Food will do."

Ada is not satisfied with this response, and argues that Food is not a solid concept to even make. Does Fiddler know a plain Food recipe? Of course not.

Fiddler chimes in after being mentioned - "Now, now, little bird, we have lots of kinds of Food. What would you or your friends like, hmm?"

Marble and Spruce contemplate for a bit, and decide they want something with Nuts. Ada likes this idea a lot, and Fiddler puts together a mixture with all kinds of Nuts, including its favorite, Almonds. They all sit down to enjoy in good spirits.
